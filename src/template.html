Game Rules

  • Drag and drop the ships on your board.
  • Two players secretly place ships of varying lengths on a grid.
  • Both players can not see the fleet of the other, you have to guess where is the ideal position to shoot at!
  • Players take turns "firing" at coordinates on the opponent's grid.
  • A "hit" is marked as "RED" when a shot strikes a ship; a "miss" is marked as "BLUE".
  • A ship is sunk when all its positions have been hit.
  • First player to sink all the opponent's ships wins the game!
